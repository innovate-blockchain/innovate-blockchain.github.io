Mission

Our mission is to create a global organization that directly advances blockchain technology from an educational standpoint. By encouraging innovation through proper education, we hope to advance the next generation of doers in the blockchain space. Some of the largest problems facing blockchain today all point to a lack of proper education and understanding for the average person. Instead, it has become a secluded community of those who "got in early" leaving the rest behind. We aim to change this paradigm by becoming the driving force behind blockchain education.

Community

We have found that the best way to build a program that achieves this grand mission, is to build a large and engaged community of doers. By organizing everyone into local charters, we provide people with an outlet in which they can openly engage ion conversation with others about the fundamental interworkings behind blockchaion technology. It allows for the open distribution of proper information, so that people are not forced to learn by their own means which can often be difficult and intimidating. Any successful program is only as strong as the community that supports it.

Roadmap

Our first charter is at Arizona State University, where we have been able to impliment a curriculum consisting of lectures, guest speakers and committee meetings. We are expanding the Blockchain Innovation Society with charters in 5+ universities in the Fall of 2018, and will continue to expand into international universities as well. Over the course of the year, the Blockchain Innovation Society will create content in the forms of lectures, blog posts, videos and other forms to be made publicly available as we grow our community.

Committees

The Blockchain Innovation Society is made up of four core committees in which we operate. These committees are R&D, investment, education and consulting. We also have an advanced group that assists with more applied projects requiring a higher level of skill and understanding.These committees are led by the current leaders, and meet with members outside of the general meetings. These committees are meant to represent the diversity within the blockhain ecosystem, helping students find which area interests them the most and focus on improving their skills in that area.

Research & Development

This committee researches new advances in the field, both at the protocol level and the application level. In addition, those who are developers, or are aspiring developers, will be working on creating and testing the ideas from our research and consulting committee
